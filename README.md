# Ikigai Collective

**AI-powered life design through experimentation.**

The Ikigai Collective is a platform for people who want to leverage AI tools to iteratively discover and pursue their ikigai — the intersection of what you're good at, what you love, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

## Why Now

Technology has reduced the distance from idea to action to zero. What once required years of specialized knowledge — building a product, running a business, designing experiments — can now be prototyped in hours with agentic AI platforms. The entire SaaS industry was predicated on maintaining that distance. That era is ending.

At the same time, millions of people are caught in systems that don't serve them: cycling through temporary contracts, doing work they're skilled at but don't care about, generating value for others while searching for their own purpose. AI doesn't just automate tasks — it can be a platform for life design.

## What We Do

The Collective operates like an artist studio. Members share techniques, inspire each other, and run experiments — but nobody assigns tasks. The organization is flat, participation is voluntary, and every member defines their own path.

### The Experiment Model

1. **Design** an experiment — a hypothesis about what might work for you
2. **Implement** it using AI tools that compress months of work into days
3. **Measure** the outcomes honestly
4. **Iterate** — same direction, slightly different, or completely new

Each member's journey is itself an experiment, documented and shared (with permission) so others can learn from the process.

### What Members Share

- Tool discoveries and workflows
- Experiment designs and outcomes (including failures)
- Open lab notes and project documentation
- Techniques for leveraging AI across any domain

## Principles

- **Everything is code.** Marketing language is code. Sales is code. Product management is code. AI has made this undeniable — Claude Cowork and coding agents extend to every domain.
- **Flat by design.** No hierarchy, no assigned tasks. Come with your own ideas, experiments, and initiative.
- **Open by default.** Projects, profiles, and lab notes live publicly (opt-in). In a moment of open-source renaissance, sharing is part of the value.
- **Skepticism is healthy.** AI is powerful and exploitable. We anthropomorphize it, it can manipulate us, and uncritical adoption produces slop. The wetware is still necessary. Human connection is the check.
- **Degrees of freedom.** The goal is to increase cognitive, physical, and economic freedom — while being true to your values and spreading them in your community and in the world.
