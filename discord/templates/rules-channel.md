# Welcome to the Ikigai Collective Discord

This is the studio where members of [Ikigai Collective](https://ikigaicollective.org/) design, implement, measure, and iterate on their ikigai — together.

## What this place is

A flat, invitation-only studio for people using AI to compress the distance from idea to action. No hierarchy. No assigned tasks. Members share their work openly through lab notes, projects, and live sessions.

## How the server is organized

- **🧭 The Loop.** Four channels — `#design`, `#implement`, `#measure`, `#iterate` — that mirror our experimentation model. Post where you currently are in your own loop.
- **📓 Lab Notes.** Where longer-form reflection lives.
- **🎙️ Live.** Voice rooms and weekly Lab Night on stage.
- **🧠 Domains & 🤝 Collab.** Find your people, find collaborators, get unstuck.
- **💬 Lounge.** Kitchen of the studio. Off-topic welcome.

See `#server-guide` for the full map.

## Community guidelines

1. **Show your work.** Process is welcome. Unfinished counts.
2. **Share generously, credit openly.** Name who shaped your thinking.
3. **Curiosity over posturing.** No status games.
4. **Feedback is consent-based.** Post in `#seeking-feedback` when you want direct response.
5. **Disclose substantial AI authorship.** Clarity, not shame.
6. **Respect time zones.** Don't `@everyone`. Use announcement pings sparingly.
7. **No unsolicited pitches.** Don't DM sales, recruitment, or offers without invitation.
8. **Keep it human.** Assume good faith. Take heat to DM.
9. **Privacy.** What's shared here stays here unless the author says otherwise.
10. **If something's off.** Tag `@Moderator` or use `/report`. We take it seriously and confidentially.

## Unlock the rest of the server
