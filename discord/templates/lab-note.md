# Lab Note — suggested structure

A shared format for `#lab-notes` posts. Not mandatory. It's here because a loose template makes posting easier, not harder — members don't have to decide the shape from scratch each time.

Copy this into a new thread in `#lab-notes` and replace the brackets.

---

**Title:** [short, specific — name the experiment, not the topic]

**Stage:** [Designing / Implementing / Measuring / Iterating]

**Domain tags:** [e.g. writing, code, music]

---

## The hypothesis

What were you curious about? What did you think might happen?

## What you did

The actual work. Tools, approach, shortcuts, dead ends. Screenshots and snippets welcome. Link to the repo / doc / file if there is one.

## What happened

Outcomes. Be specific. Numbers if they exist. Honest reactions if they don't.

## What you learned

The load-bearing part. What updated your model of the world? What surprised you?

## What you're trying next

The seed of the next loop. One sentence is fine.
